The goal of the game is to guess a number, which consists of unique digits.

When you try to guess, you find out the count of rams ans sheep.

Rams are digits which are both present in the number and occupy a correct position.

Sheep are digits which are present in the number but occupy a wrong position.

You win the game when you guess all 4 rams.

Solve the game in a fewest number of moves and take your place on a leaderboard!
